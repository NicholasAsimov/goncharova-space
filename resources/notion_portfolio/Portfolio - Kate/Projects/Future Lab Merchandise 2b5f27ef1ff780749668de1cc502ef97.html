Job Info

As a Designer, I contributed to both product experiences and brand identity by supporting the design of merchandise and promotional materials. Leveraging my background in Graphic Design, I created cohesive visuals across a variety of branded items, including T-shirts, socks, water bottles, business cards, stickers, tote bags, notepads, and posters.

My role combined product-focused thinking with hands-on graphic design, ensuring that Future Lab’s merchandise not only looked visually engaging but also aligned with the company’s values and user-centered approach to design.

Tools I Used in This Role

  • Adobe Illustrator:

    Produced scalable vector designs for merchandise such as apparel, business cards, and tote bags. Illustrator enabled precise, production-ready artwork consistent with Future Lab’s visual identity.

  • Adobe Photoshop:

    Designed and enhanced posters, stickers, and realistic product mockups. Photoshop ensured accurate color, texture, and layout representation before production.
